Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02-06-2026

QuickWA Sender respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

Information We Collect

QuickWA Sender does not collect, sell, or share your personal information.

The app may use the phone number and message text entered by you only to create a WhatsApp chat link. This information is not stored on our server.

Saved Quick Messages

If you save quick messages inside the app, they are stored locally on your device. These messages are used only inside the app so you can reuse them later.

We do not access, upload, or share your saved messages.

WhatsApp Integration

QuickWA Sender opens WhatsApp using a generated WhatsApp link. The actual message is sent only after you confirm and send it inside WhatsApp.

This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by WhatsApp or Meta.

Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties.

Internet Usage

The app may open WhatsApp or a WhatsApp web link. Any data handled after opening WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is not designed to knowingly collect data from children. Since we do not collect personal data, no child data is stored by us.

Security

Saved quick messages are stored locally on your device. Please protect your device to keep your local data safe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date.
